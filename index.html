Who is Makoy?

A rider driven by the love of open roads, changing landscapes, and the quiet freedom that comes with every journey. Passionate about exploring nature, discovering new places, and documenting real, unfiltered experiences along the way. Each ride is more than just travel—it’s a story, a moment, and a connection between the road, the environment, and the rider behind the handlebars.

What to expect

Detailed ride logs from real journeys, scenic stops worth slowing down for, and carefully chosen route recommendations for fellow riders. Expect cinematic travel moments captured on the road, honest reflections from each trip, and practical insights that blend adventure, nature, and the raw experience of riding through different places.
